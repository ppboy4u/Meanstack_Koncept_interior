Our Timeline

Some Of The Short Stories About The Journey From The Beginning Till Now.

  • March 2014

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We started our interior and designing projects basically from a home-based plan. We got our first project on "Farm House Interior Designing " which we almost completed in the specific time alloted, from there we came to an idea of increasing our business to an Office Level.

  • March 2015

    A Perfect Work-Place was Born

    We finally came to live on what we planned after one year of our very first project.We set up our main base station at M.G Road.

  • December 2016

    Transition to Full Service

    It's now so hard for us to work alone with so many projects on line, so we started hiring some more of professionals in the fields of interior and desings to work under us. Hence in making a full service plan, to always be ready for any kind of project.

  • August 2017

    Phase Two Expansion

    Now with the constant delivery of more and more successful projects, it's finally time for us to spread and expand our company over the city. So we decided to open-up more offices in few areas including Hauz-Khas , Lok Kalyan Marg, Connaught Place and few more under construction.
